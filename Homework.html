Leopard Gecko Info

Leopard geckos are small, hardy reptiles. This page lists simple, essential facts and tips.

See Quick Facts

Quick Facts

Habitat

Warm side and cool side. Simple hides. Paper towel or tile is easy for beginners.

Diet

Insects like crickets or mealworms. Provide water and calcium dust.

Temperament

Calm and easy to handle if approached gently.

Tips

Heating

Use an under-tank heat pad with a thermostat for safety.

Cleaning

Spot clean often and wipe surfaces regularly.
